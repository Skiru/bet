# Sport Protocols — Challenger Reference

## §6.5 Upset Risk Assessment
Score EVERY candidate. Record: `UPSET: [X/Y] — [top 3 factors]`.
High upset → ML BANNED, statistical markets only.
**Paradox:** High upset → OVER premium (competitive). Low upset → UNDER bias (blowout).

### Football Upset Factors (score /10)
- Relegation desperation (+2), Cup match (+1), Derby (+1)
- Away form > Home form (+1), Key absence (+2), Coach bounce (+1)
- Recent momentum (5+ unbeaten: +1), Season stage (+1), Weather equalizer (+1)

### Tennis Upset Factors (score /8)
- Surface specialist underdog (+2), Ranking close (<20 gap: +1)
- Fitness/scheduling fatigue (+2), H2H positive for underdog (+1)
- Return from injury (form unknown: +1), Tournament stage pressure (+1)

### Basketball Upset Factors (score /8)
- Back-to-back game (+2), Injury to star player (+2)
- Playoff seeding locked (+1), Rivalry game (+1)
- Home court (large: +1), Rest advantage (+1)

## §7.3 Red Flags (instant — fire = REJECT/DOWNGRADE)
- WC/Q/LL entry (tennis) → binary outcome, downgrade O22.5+
- Debut/stand-in player → stats invalid
- Coach changed <5 games ago → form data unreliable
- Key player absent (>25% team output) → recalculate
- Dead rubber (both teams nothing to play for) → corners/fouls penalty
- Extreme weather → adjust stat expectations
- Scheduling: <48h between games → fatigue adjustment

## §7.4 Contrarian Thinking (4 questions, EVERY pick)
1. Am I applying the right MODEL to this SPECIFIC case?
2. What's the #1 way this bet type LOSES?
3. Would I take it FRESH at CURRENT odds? (defeat anchoring)
4. What would a sharp disagree-er say?

## §7.5 Pick Approval Gate (18 points)
1. Identity verified 2. WC/Q/LL checked 3. H2H ≥5 4. Injuries checked
5. ≥2 sources 6. ≥1 tipster argument 7. Upset risk scored 8. EV > 0
9. Drift <8% 10. Red flags checked 11. Contrarian done 12. Bear < bull
13. Not anchored 14. 48h repeat check 15. Multi-market comparison
16. H2H stat-specific 17. Three-way alignment 18. Data quality sufficient
ALL 18 PASS → APPROVED. ANY fail → REJECT/DOWNGRADE/WATCHLIST.
